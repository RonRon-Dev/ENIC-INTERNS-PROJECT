# Dashboard Overview

After logging in, you land on the **Dashboard**. What you see here depends on your role — not all sections are visible to all users.

## Sidebar Navigation

The left sidebar is your main navigation. It is organized into groups:

- **Home** — always accessible to all roles
- **Operations** — contains the Data Cleaning Tool (for `operations`, `admin`, and higher)
- **Users** — user management (for `admin` and `superadmin` only)
- **Settings** — account and system settings

::: tip
The sidebar collapses on smaller screens. Click the menu icon in the top left to toggle it.
:::

## Your Account

Your name and role are shown at the bottom of the sidebar. Click it to access account settings or log out.
