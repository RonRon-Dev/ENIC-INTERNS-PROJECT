# Logging In

## Accessing the System

Open your browser and navigate to the ENIC MIS URL provided by your team.

## Signing In

1. Enter your **username** (e.g. `j.luna`)
2. Enter your **password** (e.g. `S3cur3P@ssw0rd`)
3. Click **Login**

::: tip First-time login
If this is your first time, your supervisor will give you a temporary password. Use it to log in — you'll be prompted to change it immediately.
:::

## Forgot Your Password?

1. Click **Forgot Password?** on the login screen
2. Enter your username and submit
3. A reset request is sent to your administrator
4. Once approved, a new temporary password will be provided to you

::: info
Password reset requests require admin approval. If your request is taking too long, contact your supervisor directly.
:::

## Common Login Issues

| Problem | What to Do |
|---|---|
| "Invalid credentials" | Check your username and password — passwords are case-sensitive |
| Account not found | Your account may not be approved yet — contact your supervisor |
| Account locked | Too many failed attempts — ask your admin to unlock your account |
| Pending approval | Your registration hasn't been approved yet — wait for admin action |
